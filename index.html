Connecting ambitious high school students with the people and opportunities that help them grow.

Our Mission

At LinkedTeen, our mission is to empower high school students to build meaningful connections that support their interests, education, and real-world skills.

We aim to create a student-focused network where teens can discover opportunities, collaborate with motivated peers, and access mentorship in a safe and supportive environment.

Built by students. For students.
